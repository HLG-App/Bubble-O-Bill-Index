What is this, actually?
Explain it like I'm five
Imagine you had $10 and a Bubble O' Bill cost $2. You could buy 5 ice creams.

Now imagine your $10 grew to $12. Sounds great! But now the ice cream costs $4. You can only buy 3 ice creams.

In dollars, you got richer. In ice creams, you got poorer.

That's inflation. The number goes up. But what it buys you quietly goes down. This tool shows you both. Because the system only ever shows you one.
